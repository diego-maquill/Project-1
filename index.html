About nuBie

NuBie specializes in two main components of the relocation process, logistics and adjustment. During critical logistic decision-making points of your relocation, NuBie provides integrated access to the most reliable data sources for areas such as housing and careers. NuBie takes relocation one step further with multiple product offerings to support the adjustment period with life essentials such as building a social network and knowledge of the food landscape in your new home.
